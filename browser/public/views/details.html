Ceremony and Reception

When: September 17th, 2016

Where: Women's Club of Englewood Carriage House, Englewood NJ

In order to escape NYC real estate madness, we decided to head across the George Washington Bridge to the historic town of Englewood. The Ceremony and reception will both take place at the historic carriagehouse built for the first mayor of Englewood.

Getting There

german shepherd checks

Flying

All the NYC airports are well-connected to public transportation. If you are staying in NJ, we recommend flying into Newark . It is about a 30 min drive, and doesn't require you to drive across Manhattan

From NYC

There are busses from Port Authority (NJ Transit bus 166) and the GW Bridge Bus Terminal (NJ Transit bus 186), which is as expected, near the entrance to the GW Bridge on 178th and Forth Washington

Our Story

1993

When her friends wanted to play house, she insisted on being the cat.

2002: She became obsessed with ski racing. It is the closest she can get to flying.

2006-2011: She caught the travel bug, and proceded to leave the country whenever possible, doing various forms of study-abroad in France, Croatia, Morocco, and Palestine.

May 2012: She moved to New York to teach science in a middle school in East Harlem.

Nov 2012: While procrastinating on grading papers, she decided to check out that online dating theing everyone kept talking about.

Jan 2013: He caught her attention on OK Cupid for smelling old books.

Jan 18, 2013: At the end of their first date, he suggested a walk, and after walking 80 blocks up the Hudson River in the frigid January weather, her legs were almost numb, but they both realized there might be something there.

Jun 2015: She decided to quit teaching and become a software engineer

Apr 2015: She finally convinced him to come travelling with her. They decided to go to Aruba.

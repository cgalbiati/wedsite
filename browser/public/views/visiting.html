Our favorites in NYC

The NYC Transit Museum chronicles the history of public transit in NYC, with exibits on the construction of the subways, as well as cars dating back to elevated trains in the mid-1800's

The Tennament Museum has tells the history of a tenament building in the Lower East Side, reconstructing the stories and apparments of tennants through the history of the building

The High Line is a park built on a section of abandoned elevated railway in Chelsea

A classic, the American Museum of Natural History has dinosaurs. Also a big flying whale.
